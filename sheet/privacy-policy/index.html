Privacy Policy
Intruduction

This privacy policy describes how Rephelps.com("we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, and stores your("user", "you" or "your") data. We do not take responsibility in any of the purchased goods that were purchased through the links on our website.

Data we collect

Uploaded images: while using our websites table of items you are able to upload images to search for similiar results.
Search prompts: while using our websites table of items you are able to search for keywords to find items.

How we use the data

The data is used in third party services(described below) to help you find the best matches to your searches. Your uploaded images are only stored in memory throughout the duration of the request, however your search prompts are stored stored for 3 days to find any anomalies and fix issues in the website

Third party services

Amazon Web Services (AWS): Our API endpoints are hosted on AWS, which means that Website Content as well as Uploaded Images are transmitted to and processed by AWS servers.
MongoDB: While searching, the search prompts are used here to query through all the items, and find what you need.
Google Cloud Platform: When you try to image search with uploaded images using our table, the photo will be classified using Google Cloud.
Pinecone: The classified images are used here to find the closest match in our database
